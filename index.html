Penalties
⚠️
Incident Penalty
−1 championship point for every 3 incidents accumulated across the season. Rounded down (e.g. 7 incidents = −2 pts). Tracked separately from the per-round Clean Race bonus.
🛑
Pit Window Violation
+30 seconds added to race result for pitting before Lap 3 or after the 50-minute mark. Applied manually by admin before standings are published.
